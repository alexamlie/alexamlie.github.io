Hello! I'm Alex, a PhD computational biologist with 7 years of biotech experience at <a href="https://arrakistx.com" target="_blank">Arrakis Therapeutics</a>, where I worked on RNA-targeting small molecule drug discovery. My expertise spans human genetics, multi-omics integration, RNA biology, statistical genetics, and genomics platform development. I received my PhD from the <a href="https://www.med.upenn.edu/gcb/" target="_blank">Genomics and Computational Biology</a> program at the University of Pennsylvania in <a href="https://lisanwanglab.org" target="_blank">Li-San Wang's lab</a>, where I developed <a href="https://bitbucket.org/alexamlie/inferno/" target="_blank">INFERNO</a>, an open-source framework for variant-to-gene-to-function inference applied to neurological diseases and population-scale biobank data.
